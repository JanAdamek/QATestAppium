Saving Policy On Demand to your Mobile Home Screen

How to save Policy On Demand to your iOS Home Screen

  • Open up Safari on your iOS device.
  • Navigate to Policy On Demand https://insidetaxpolicy.pwc.com to save as a home screen shortcut.
  • Tap the Share button on the menu bar. It’s an icon on the right side that’s a box with an arrow sticking out from it.
  • Tap on Add to Home Screen.
  • On the next page you’ll give the shortcut a name and confirm the web address.
  • After that, tap on Add in the upper-right corner to add the shortcut to your home screen.

How to save Policy On Demand to your Android Home Screen

  • Open up Chrome on your Android device.
  • Navigate to Policy On Demand https://policyondemand.pwc.com to save as a home screen shortcut.
  • Tap the menu button to Add to Homescreen.
  • On the next page you’ll give the shortcut a name and confirm the web address.
  • The shortcut is now added to your home screen.
